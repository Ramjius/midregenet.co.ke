    Popular dish of Ethiopia. It is a spicy and flavorful chicken stew that is highly cherished.

  • Key Wot

    Key Wot is a traditional Ethiopian and Eritrean dish. It is a rich and spicy stew made with tender chunks of beef simmered in a flavorful sauce.

  • Normal Kitfo

    Kitfo is a minced raw or lightly cooked beef dish seasoned with a blend of spices. The beef is typically marinated with spices like mitmita (a spicy chili powder), salt, garlic, and sometimes cardamom and other aromatic herbs.

  • Kitfo with Kotcho

    Kitfo is a lightly cooked beef dish seasoned with a blend of spices. Kotcho, on the other hand is made from finely chopped, seasoned collard greens.

  • Shekla Tibs

    Grilled or sautéed meat and vegetables, served with injera, a sourdough flatbread, or other bread options.

  • Doro (Chicken) Shekla

    Marinated chicken with spices and seasonings that is cooked on the shekla pan. The shekla's unique cooking method imparts a smoky flavor to the chicken, adding depth and richness to the dish.

  • Shiro

    Shiro is a popular Ethiopian and Eritrean dish that consists of a thick stew made from ground legumes, such as chickpeas, lentils, or a combination of both.

  • Missir Wot

    Missir Wot, also known as Misir Wot or Mesir Wot, is a popular Ethiopian and Eritrean dish that is made from red lentils.

Today’s Best Offer

Indulge in the authentic taste of Ethiopia with our featured dish,Doro Wot. Tender chicken pieces are simmered in a rich and aromatic sauce, carefully crafted with a blend of traditional spices and berbere, the fiery Ethiopian spice mixture. This iconic dish offers a harmonious balance of flavors, with hints of garlic, ginger, fenugreek, and other aromatic spices.

Served alongside our freshly made injera, a spongy and tangy Ethiopian flatbread, Doro Wot is a complete culinary experience that will transport you to the vibrant streets of Addis Ababa. The tender chicken, infused with the fragrant sauce, is perfect for dipping into the injera or enjoyed with a side of our flavorful vegetable dishes.
